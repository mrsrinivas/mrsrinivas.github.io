I'm **Srinivas Reddy**, a Cloud Data and ML Engineering Lead with 12+ years of experience building large-scale data platforms, real-time streaming systems, and machine learning infrastructure.

Currently I lead data and ML engineering at **Constantinople**, where I architect cloud-native pipelines, feature platforms, and model serving infrastructure that power core product and risk decisioning.

## Background

Before Constantinople, I was an **Engineering Lead at Grab** — Southeast Asia's superapp — where I led data platform teams responsible for petabyte-scale data processing, real-time event streaming, and ML feature stores serving hundreds of millions of users across ride-hailing, food delivery, and fintech verticals.

Prior to Grab I held engineering and lead roles at **Accenture**, **Model N**, **Verizon**, and **Tech4sys**, working across industries including telecom, revenue management, and enterprise software.

I hold a **Master's degree from the National University of Singapore (NUS)**.

## What I Work On

My focus sits at the intersection of data engineering, distributed systems, and applied ML:

- **Streaming architectures** — Kafka, Spark Structured Streaming, Flink for sub-second latency pipelines
- **Cloud data platforms** — data lakes, lakehouses, and warehouse systems on AWS, GCP, and Azure
- **ML infrastructure** — feature stores, model registries, online/offline serving, experiment tracking
- **Platform engineering** — self-serve data tooling, observability, and reliability for data teams

## Writing

This blog is where I share hard-won lessons from production systems — architecture decisions, performance trade-offs, and the operational realities of running data infrastructure at scale.
